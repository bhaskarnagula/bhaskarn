All the specialties requires Professional expertise in coding and billing for the services rendered. Because every specialty not only has different guidelines for billing but every insurance treats these services in different manner and they have their own set of billing protocols for reimbursement. Medicare has altogether different set of rules and guidelines for billing So if the services are not billed as per the guidelines, not only you do risk the timely reimbursement, but sometimes you have to write off the billing amount.

Our multi-specialty AAPC Certified coders are expert in billing and coding based on Medicare and commercial insurance guidelines and protocols and can assure you the correct billing and reimbursement for your services.
